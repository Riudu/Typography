My recent trip to Santiniketan

in 20th December, 2024

-Shouryadeep Das

20th December, 2024

I woke up at 6:00 am not only at the fact that my school closed for Christmas Holidays, but also at the fact that I will be visiting Santiniketan. My father hired a Car driver for our road trip. It was a 5 hr. long road trip, so we stopped for a cup of tea half-way. The distance between Kolkata and Bolpur(Santiniketan) by road is 185 km. At about 11:30 am, we arrived at our Bungalow. We had our lunch at the hotel next door. After lunch we set out on road for the Museum. There, in the Museum, we saw all about Kaviguru Rabindranath Tagore and his personal life. We also saw many 'Gitanjali' books in various languages like Russian, German, French, and so on and so forth. We also saw various things from foreign countries used by Rabindranath and Rabindranath himself with various famous personalities like:

Albert Einstein
a scientist famous for his theory of relativity, his equation E=mc2, and his contributions to quantum mechanics
Helen Keller
A world-famous author famous for overcoming the challenges of blindness and deafness to become a leading advocate for people with disabilities, and for being the first deaf-blind person to earn a college degree
Mahatma Gandhi
A crucial figure famous for his nonviolent resistance to British rule in India, his use of non-cooperation and peaceful protest, and his ability to unite people of different faiths
Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose
A freedom fighter,famous for his role in India's independence movement, including leading the INA, advocating for socialist policies, Coining the patriotic greeting 'Jai Hind' and delivering the speech 'Give me Blood, and I Will Give You Freedom'.
Jawaharlal Nehru
the 1st president of India, famous for leading India to Independence, promoting a pluralistic democracy, establishing the non-aligned movement and balancing traitionalism with a more secular perspective.

after that we rested at the bungalow where I spent my time watching TV. after that we had our dinner (which we had to buy from outside because the bungalow we stayed in did not have a cafeteria) at 8:00 pm and went to bed at 8:30 pm for our next tours.




21st December, 2024

I woke up at 6:00 am again because we were going to SJH. I quickly had my breakfast and went with my family there. There I saw a ton of shops set up in a Forest area. We bought some things and went for lunch at Shakuntala Hotel. After finishing lunch, we went to Srijoni Shilpa mela. There we saw some more shops set up and a demon mascot. We also saw some live statues and some Bauls performing on stage. After all that, we went back to our Bungalow, had dinner and went to bed at 8:30 pm as tomorrow was our return trip back to Kolkata.




22nd December, 2024

I woke up at 5:00 am this time and had a biscuit. After that we packed up and loaded all our luggage into the car and went on our return trip. During our return trip, we went to Barkreswar, where we took a bath in piping hot water and worshipped in a temple. After that, we had our lunch and got on our way. We had to pass by many diversions, Because of this, we reached home at 6:00 pm. At home we had our lunch at 10:00 pm and went to bed.
